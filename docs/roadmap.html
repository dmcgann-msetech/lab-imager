  • Do not refactor working capture, preview, recording, Notes, or PDF export systems unless required to resolve warnings.
  • Prefer narrow warning fixes over broad code reshaping.
  • Do not remove diagnostic tools unless their replacement is confirmed.
  • Validate with dotnet build after every cleanup pass.
  • Preserve modular architecture.
