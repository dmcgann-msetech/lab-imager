# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Lab Imager Open Source?

No.

Lab Imager is proprietary software developed by MSE McGann Systems Engineering.

---

## What Cameras Are Supported?

Any DirectShow-compatible camera or capture device.

Examples:

* USB microscopes
* HDMI capture cards
* USB cameras

---

## What File Formats Are Generated?

Current formats include:

* PNG
* HTML evidence reports
* AVI recordings

Future formats may include:

* PDF evidence packages

---

## Where Are Captures Stored?

Captures are stored in the configured evidence output location.

Future versions may allow custom output-folder configuration.

---

## Can I Record Video?

Yes.

Current recording support uses AVI output.

---

## Can I Add Notes To Captures?

Yes.

Session notes and metadata can be included as part of the evidence workflow.

---

## Why Doesn't My Camera Appear?

Verify:

* Device is connected
* Device drivers are installed
* Device appears in Device Manager
* Device is not in use by another application

---

## Why Is Preview Not Starting?

Verify:

* Camera selected
* Format selected
* Device available
* Preview service initialized successfully

---

## What Is Freeze Frame?

Freeze locks the current image for inspection while retaining the active camera connection.

Resume returns the viewport to live preview.

---

## How Do I Report A Problem?

Document:

* Application version
* Windows version
* Camera model
* Capture format
* Steps to reproduce the issue

Include screenshots whenever possible.
